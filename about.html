About 1 in 56 Million

Who we are, what we believe, and the limits we hold ourselves to.

1 in 56 Million exists for people who suspect their family carries a thread back to ancient Israel — and who want to explore that honestly, without being told what to believe.

What the name means

“1 in 56 million” is the long odds a person might beat to trace a living line back to a specific ancient ancestor — and a nod to how improbable, and how personal, this search feels.

What we do

We share the history and the claims of British-Israelism, point to real genealogical and genetic research, and offer a free DNA kit as an exploratory first step.

What we don’t claim

We do not tell you that you are an Israelite. British-Israelism is disputed by mainstream scholarship; we present it as a heritage theory to explore, and we are explicit that DNA cannot prove descent from a lost tribe.

How it’s funded

The kit is currently free. If that ever changes, we’ll say so plainly on this page.
