How It Works

From the form to your result, step by step — and what the test can and can’t say.

The process is simple, free, and exploratory. Here is exactly what happens and what you get back.

1. Request your kit

Fill in the form with your name and a mailing address so we can post the kit. That’s all we need from you.

2. Swab and return

A painless cheek swab, sealed in the prepaid envelope, goes directly to the partner laboratory. We never handle or see your DNA.

3. The lab analyses it

The lab produces an ancestry and ethnicity estimate, including any markers associated with Middle-Eastern or Israelite-linked heritage.

4. You get a result

You receive an ancestry indicator. It is a research lead — not proof of tribal descent, Jewish status, or Law-of-Return eligibility. What a DNA test can and cannot show is explained in full on our DNA testing guide.
